The Next Generation of Computing

Where things are headed, and my plans for 2022 onwards.

Tablets are the new laptops/thin-clients.

With the advent of GitHub Codespaces, VSCode, StackBlitz, and others, its very clear where the modern development environment is moving. to the cloud!

Imagine a scenario where companies no longer need to supply employees with ultra-super-powerful-mega computing devices. All expensive processing is handled in a private cloud and streamed back to the accessing thin-client.

What would cost a company roughly ~2k for a new Macbook Pro, can now achieved with a low-end laptop or tablet device. Repairing and maintaining company equipment is reduced by a large factor, and can be easily replaced without breaking the bank. This is especially valuable when it comes to small business/startups.
